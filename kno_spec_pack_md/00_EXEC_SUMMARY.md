# KNO Post‑Purchase Surveys – Executive Summary

## What KNO does, who it’s for and the core loop

* **Shopify‑focused post‑purchase survey platform.** KNO Post‑Purchase Surveys is a Shopify app that embeds multi‑question surveys on the **order confirmation (Thank You) and order status pages** to collect zero‑party data from customers immediately after they check out【956241798895964†L121-L153】.  
* **Designed for DTC brands and agencies.** Merchants using Shopify (from Basic to Plus) install the app to get **insights into how customers discovered their brand, what motivated the purchase and how long the buyer considered**【364980791551066†L90-L109】. Agencies and media buyers use the data to evaluate channel performance.  
* **Template‑driven survey builder.** KNO offers a **5‑minute setup with 30+ full survey templates and dozens of pre‑built questions**【367261123567251†L120-L139】, plus the option to start from scratch. The builder allows merchants to add questions (radio, checkbox, text, scale, dropdown, etc.) and customise wording and styles while staying within their plan limits【451442093169152†L32-L53】【881960173322908†L36-L118】.  
* **Logic flows and follow‑ups.** Questions can use **conditional logic** so that follow‑up questions or **Actions** (e.g., coupon codes or review prompts) only display based on earlier answers【945476463987832†L69-L90】. Logic settings live on the follow‑up question, allowing multiple branching paths and exit points【945476463987832†L41-L99】.  
* **Audience targeting & sequencing.** Merchants create **Audiences** based on order/customer attributes (e.g., first‑time vs returning buyers, product purchased, location, language, survey completed)【607622501565733†L47-L107】. When multiple surveys are live, KNO’s **survey display logic** randomly picks a survey that matches the customer and supports mutually exclusive, sequential or round‑robin survey experiences【132019224187076†L60-L123】.  
* **Thank‑you page app block & link distribution.** Surveys can be delivered through the Shopify **Checkout UI extension** (Thank You/Order Status pages) by adding a KNO app block in the checkout editor【734100394826036†L190-L214】. Surveys can also be shared via unique URLs or QR codes to capture feedback outside checkout; merchants can force email capture on link surveys to tie responses to customer profiles【985696967857397†L66-L124】.  
* **Reporting & attribution modelling.** The app provides **Survey stats**, **core reports** and an **Order Attribution report** that link survey responses to order count, revenue and average order value. It estimates total orders/revenue by applying response rates to the entire order cohort【954105040073377†L54-L101】【954105040073377†L162-L183】. Reports allow filtering by survey, question, audience and date range.  
* **Integrations and extensibility.** KNO integrates with **Klaviyo**, **Shopify Flow**, **ReCharge**, **Triple Whale**, **Peel Insights**, **Google Analytics**, **TikTok**, and other tools【978605946208732†L121-L123】. Survey responses and status can be pushed into Klaviyo as custom properties【959380932338212†L144-L179】, and triggers can be used in Shopify Flow to tag orders or customers【15588629035657†L36-L47】【15588629035657†L273-L310】.  
* **Post‑survey actions & incentives.** Beyond data collection, KNO offers **Actions** such as one‑time promo codes, review prompts, referral invites or social follow CTAs. Actions are inserted into the survey flow and can use logic to display only for certain answers【672473035596646†L36-L69】. The **Promo Code Action** automatically generates one‑time or multi‑use discount codes in Shopify and applies them when customers click through【295004260503847†L77-L104】【295004260503847†L160-L177】.  
* **Automated insights & benchmarking.** Higher‑tier plans include **Automated Insights**, a dashboard that aggregates demographic and psychographic responses (age, gender, spending habits, social networks) and offers benchmarking against other brands【624157833394015†L40-L87】【624157833394015†L116-L130】.  
* **Plans & limits.** Pricing tiers (Starter, Analyst, Pro) cap the number of surveys, questions and audiences and unlock additional question types, actions and integrations【978605946208732†L200-L249】. Starter plans support radio/checkbox/text questions; Analyst adds dropdown, slider, NPS, date picker, ranking and email/phone capture; Pro unlocks image/video upload questions【881960173322908†L36-L118】.

## Differentiators / Unique elements

1. **Multi‑channel survey delivery with zero‑party data capture.** Unlike many survey tools, KNO embeds surveys **directly in the Shopify checkout (Thank You/Order Status) via a Checkout UI extension**, ensuring high response rates and linking responses to orders【734100394826036†L190-L214】.  
2. **Advanced audience targeting using 60+ order/customer attributes.** Merchants can build audiences using customer lifetime order count, location, product IDs, discount codes and even previous survey responses【607622501565733†L69-L119】. Survey display logic supports mutually exclusive, sequential and round‑robin survey experiences【132019224187076†L60-L121】.  
3. **Action engine to drive immediate engagement.** The ability to reward respondents with **auto‑generated promo codes, review prompts, referral invites or social follow CTAs** within the same survey widget differentiates KNO from simple feedback collectors【672473035596646†L46-L69】.  
4. **Order‑level attribution reporting and estimation.** KNO’s **Order Attribution report** ties survey answers to order data, computes response rate and can project revenue/orders as if 100 % of customers responded【954105040073377†L96-L183】. This modelling helps marketers estimate channel revenue beyond raw response counts.  
5. **Deep integrations and automation hooks.** Native integration with **Klaviyo**, **Shopify Flow**, and other analytics platforms enables pushing survey responses into customer profiles, triggering automations (e.g., tagging orders or sending Slack messages) and populating dashboards without manual exports【959380932338212†L142-L179】【15588629035657†L36-L47】.
