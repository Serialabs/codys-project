---

## `shops`

| Field | Type | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| `id` | UUID (PK) | Internal identifier |

## Indexing Strategy

| Index | Reason |
|---|---|
| `shops.shop_domain` (unique) | Fast lookup on signed request validation |
| `surveys.shop_id`, `questions.survey_id`, `responses.survey_id` | FK join performance |
| `responses.order_id` | Revenue analysis joins |
| `audiences.survey_id` | Audience rule lookup per survey |
| `response_items.question_id` | Answer aggregation per question |
| `promo_codes.code` (unique) | Prevent duplicate codes |

---

## Data Retention

- **Responses** — Retained indefinitely. Individual responses can be deleted on GDPR/CCPA request.
- **Promo codes** — Retained until 1 year after expiration for redemption auditing.
- **Access tokens** — Encrypted at rest; rotated on reinstall or permission change.
- **Surveys and audiences** — Soft‑deleted so historical responses remain accessible.
